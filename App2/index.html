Moment Obama raps Eminem's Lose Yourself a Detriot Rally

In a memorable moment following Eminem’s introduction, former President Obama kicked off his remarks by joking that he felt nervous after Eminem’s performance—humorously reciting lines from “Lose Yourself” before declaring his love for the rapper. He then pivoted to rally the crowd, urging Detroiters to channel their energy into the ballot box, not boos, and to vote for Kamala Harris. Obama delivered a sharp critique of Donald Trump—questioning his competence, warning against unchecked power, and mocking his out-of-touch actions—before highlighting Harris’s working‑class roots, her robust policy plans, and her readiness to lead.
